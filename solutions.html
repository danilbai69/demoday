What are some solutions to global warming?

Although it will be difficult to revert the effects of global warming, there are ways we can limit it.

  • Reducing Greenhouse Gas Emissions

    • By shifting gears to electric vehicles, limiting energy usage, and utilizing renewable energy can help reduce greenhouse gases.

  • Protecting land and agriculture

    • By planting trees and banning deforestation, we can absorb more air from the atmosphere. We can promote healthy agriculture habits for farmers and protect ecosystems.

  • Even changing our everyday behavior can benefit the cause.

    • We must make sure we ride our bicycles more often, turn the lights off when we aren't in the room, and recycling materials all contribute to fighting global warming.
